We do... What we love to do...

Ornithology Study Group is a society where you can learn about birds, how they live, feed and breed. You'll also learn about bird photography which is one of the most hardest fields in photography by giving you instructions, recommending camera equipment and showing you photos taken by expert bird photographers.

So if you are passionate about birds and what we do,we warmly invite you to join our society and improve your knowledge about amazing birds in the world.You can also participate for research campaigns and field trips with bird experts..If you're interseted please contact us and be a proud member....
